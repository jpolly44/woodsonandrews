About

Two currents, one direction.

Woodson Andrews is an independent advisory firm built on a simple premise: the best outcomes come from partners who bring judgment, not just credentials.

We work alongside government agencies and private clients to navigate strategy, funding, and growth; pairing historic roots in the Midwest with an international field of view. Clients keep us close for the same reasons a good advisor earns trust anywhere: we tell them what they need (not necessarily want) to hear, move at the speed their decisions require, and stay accountable long after an engagement ends.
